F.A.Q.

Q:Please describe your career experience as a teacher of English A:I was recruited by one of my university teachers to work at her school (she was the headmistress) when I still was studying and IÂ´ve been working as a Teacher of English since then.

Q: What is unique about your professional background? A: I have learned and studied the language since my early childhood both in my country and in English-speaking countries. I went to university in Chile and in the US, I have also improved myself (courses, training, diplomas among others). I have also traveled to various countries (not just English-speaking) as an exchange student at the college level.

Q: What do you love about the English language? A: It is a common language used around the world with which we can all communicate.

Q: What are some of your favourite books written in English? A. I have quite eclectic tastes, from classics like Shakespeare and the Bronte sisters, Milton, Beatrix Potter to more modern ones.

Q: How can consuming media in English help me learn the language? A: As it is within the reach of everyone, or the vast majority, to be able to connect and search for interesting material or topics such as movies, etc.

Q: Is my child too young to start learningn English? A. I was 3 or 4 years old when I started. I guess at the same time that I was learning my mother tongue.

Q: What should I look for in any school offering a bilingual program for my children? A: That it is in the school's PEI, suitable teaching staff, school activities where English is present, among others.

Q: Am I too old to start studying English? A. Nom you are never too old.

Q: Can I learn English all by myself?, Why would I need a Teacher/Tutor? A: You can learn the basics but you will reach a certain level where you will need the support of a teacher. In addition, you must know if you are pronouncing correctly and that can only be achieved with a qualified and trained guide.

Q: I would like to pursue English (as a foreing language) as a career, What should I look for in a College? A: Know the history of the university in question, its plans and programs, its academics and graduates.

Services offered

Classes for children

I have prepared and participated in the creation of plans and programs, approved by the MINEDUC, which are being implemented in various educational establishments throughout the country. So I am very familiar with the plans and programs that are used in various schools and how and what to teach little children

Classes for adults

Many adults feel the need to learn or improve their English level in their careers. I have helped adults to improve their conversation level and technical vocabulary in engineering, military, pharmaceutical concepts

Preparation for standarized tests

Many students who wish to apply to foreign programs need to pass some standarized English tests. This tests meassure their English skills with a variety of intentions. For example, TOEIC measures your ability to work in English while the TOEFL measures your ability to take university courses in English. I'm familiar with how this tests are built and I can help you prepare for them. I can identifying the area where you most need help, wheter is speaking, writing, listening comprehension, etc
